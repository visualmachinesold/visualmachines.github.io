Visual Machines Group to Launch this Fall
UCLA welcomes Professor Kadambi and his team of students to begin research in the field of computational imaging at the Samueli School of Engineering this fall.
The Visual Machines Group Welcomes Ethan Mitchell
As the Visual Machines Group continues to expand, we welcome Ethan Mitchell to the lab. Ethan is an undergraduate Electrical Engineering student at UCLA and will begin work in the lab under Dr. Kadambi this fall. If you are interested in the Visual Machines group, click the Join Us link at the top of the page for information on how to join.
Textbook on Computational Imaging Accepted to MIT Press
It is with excitement that the Visual Machines Group congratulates Achuta Kadambi and his co-writers for their textbook's acceptance to MIT Press. The textbook titled, "Computational Time of Flight Imaging" is expected to be released this fall.
