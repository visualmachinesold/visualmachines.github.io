Visual Machines Group Opens July 1
The Visual Machines Group is established July 1 2018 at UCLA. A "visual machine" is a robot with intelligent eyesight. The group is interested in applications to autonomous vehicles and digital health (e.g. cars that drive through fog, implantable optical biosensors, etc).
